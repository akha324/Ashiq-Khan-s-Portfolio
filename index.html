Introduction

Hello! I'm Ashiq — a Computer Science student at CUNY Queens College with a passion for technology and digital innovation.
I've always been fascinated by how computers work and how software can solve real-world problems.
My interests include both front-end and back-end development, where I enjoy transforming ideas into interactive experiences.
I'm constantly exploring new programming languages, frameworks, and tools to stay ahead in a fast-moving field.
I take pride in writing clean, maintainable code and enjoy the challenge of debugging complex issues.
Whether it's building web applications or solving algorithm problems, I approach every task with curiosity, focus, and persistence.

Education & Background

I study Computer Science at CUNY Queens College and previously graduated from Thomas A. Edison CTE High School.
There, I earned an Advanced Regents Diploma and CTE endorsement in Computer Networking.
I also successfully passed the CCENT certification (Cisco Certified Entry Networking Technician), strengthening my foundation in networking.
The hands-on experience with routers and switches gave me a solid technical base.
My passion for problem-solving and innovation grew as I learned about networks and system design.
Since then, I've completed courses in Java, C++, Python, and full-stack web development.
This education has equipped me with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills for modern software engineering.
